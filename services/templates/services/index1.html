Owner Representation

{% endblock image %} {% block content %} {% endblock content %} {% block content2 %}

We work closely with prospective building owners and developers to create a seamless construction process from concept, land acquisition, and the selection of design professionals and contractors, through construction and close-out. Our services include:

  • Owner dialogue in order to ascertain needs, preliminary programming, and “best fit” analysis.
  • Assistance in determining property needs and (when applicable) assisting with the property acquisition piece.
  • Leading the efforts to narrow appropriate architectural firms, then providing the basis and matrix for the interviews and selection, as well as contract negotiations with that firm.
  • Working with the owner and architect to coordinate the architect’s programming component and schematic design potentially leading to:
    • Leading the efforts to narrow appropriate contracting firms then providing the basis and matrix for interviews and selection, as well as contract negotiations with that firm.
    • Owner-related contract administration and liaison with the architect and contractor (or construction manager) during the course of construction and oversight of pay applications, change orders, and close-out documentation.
