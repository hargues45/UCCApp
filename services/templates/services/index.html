In order to assist lenders in determining overall project feasibility and progress, we evaluate and analyze contract documents and observe and measure project progress in order to determine:

  • The cost and constructability of the project.
  • Accuracy and propriety of interim contract draw requests and back-up documentation.
  • Overall review of adherence to plans, specifications, workmanship, and generally accepted industry standards.
  • General quality assessment.
  • Accuracy and propriety of change orders, RFIs and submittals.
  • Adherence to construction schedule.
  • Final payment and close-out review.

We manage the project from start to finish. Our experience in virtually every aspect of construction makes us an invaluable construction advisor.
